About me

I am a researcher in the Theory of Machine Learning chair at University of Tübingen. Since 2018, I head the Network Analysis team of the TML group. I am also a member of the Baden-Württemberg Elitepogramm.

My primary areas of research are network analysis and learning theory. But I work on various aspects of machine learning ranging from applied research problems to theoretical questions.
