Debarghya Ghoshdastidar

Assistant Professor

Technical University of Munich

I am an assistant professor in Department of Informatics of TU Munich, where I lead the research group Theoretical Foundations of Artificial Intelligence.

My research ranges from the theory of machine learning to design of algorithms for complex learning problems. I mainly work on network analysis, statistical learning theory and high-dimensional statistics, but often explore other topics as well. For more information about my research interests, check publications or recent projects
