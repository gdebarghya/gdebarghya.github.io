Debarghya Ghoshdastidar

Assistant Professor

Technical University of Munich

I am an assistant professor in Department of Informatics of TU Munich, where I lead the research group Theoretical Foundations of Artificial Intelligence.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at University of Tübingen, and a member of the Baden-Württemberg Elitepogramm.

My primary research areas are learning theory and network analysis. But I work on various aspects of machine learning and AI ranging from applied research problems to theoretical questions.
